About Prep Campus

We believe that the first five years of a child's life is one of the most pivotal times for development and can have a lasting impact on a child's transition into adulthood. Therefore, to set a strong foundation in the early years of a child’s learning experience, we have designed an age-appropriate curriculum filled with opportunities for children to play and learn. Our innovative program is a blend of the best learning methods and techniques which caters to the holistic development of a child.

Play-Based Learning

We believe that play is an essential part of learning for young children. Our curriculum is designed to promote play-based learning, which encourages creativity, imagination, and problem-solving skills. Through play-based activities, our children explore and learn about the world around them in a fun and engaging way. Our classrooms are well equipped with child friendly resources for them to experience hands on learning.

Student-Centred Approach

With appropriate student teacher ratio we ensure to tailor to the needs of each child by giving one to one attention and foster a love of learning in our students. Our Pre-primary mentors are trained to focus on the individual needs and interests of each child, creating personalized learning experiences that cater to their unique learning styles.

Parent’s Involvement

We recognize the importance of building positive relationships with our students and their families. Our mentors work closely with parents to create a supportive and collaborative learning environment, where parents are actively involved in their child's education. Our teachers share feedbacks with the parents to discuss the progress of their children on a weekly and monthly basis.

Language Development

Early years of a child’s educational journey is a time when they begin to build relationships with their peers and teachers. Communication skills, such as the ability to listen, express themselves, and take turns during conversations, help young children to establish positive relationships with others. At the Prep campus children are encouraged to participate in activities that can develop their communication, social and emotional skills such as public speaking during the school assembly, reading books, story narration, etc…

Values and Character-building Traits

We believe that as important as it is for children to achieve academically, it is also important for children to cultivate positive characteristics from their early years and be successful in life. Here are a few character traits that our young learners are introduced to:

  • Responsibility: We encourage children to take responsibility of their actions, their belongings, and their surroundings. By encouraging them to unpacking and packing their bags in class, making the class leaders, line leaders, folding napkins, self-grooming etc.
  • Gratitude: Attitude of gratitude is a way in which children can be taught to see life in a positive way. Encouraging children to be thankful, and empathetic is any important behaviour trait we inherit in our children at Prep Campus. As a part of which we celebrate Grandparents Day, Helper’s Day, and Teacher’s Day. With their hearts filled with love and innocence children share presents and donate gifts on special days to bring joy in the lives of the privileged children.
  • Integrity: Talking to children about being honest and about doing the right thing even when no one is looking, is a trait that our children absorb in their foundational years.

Educational Trips

We believe in taking our children on educational trips that enhances their critical thinking skills and gives them a chance to think about a topic or theme from a different perspective while children love to explore the new places.

Events

Children are motivated to participate in events like Masquerade Parade, Open House to boost their morale, to be a fearless orator and to face the crowd with confidence. Children are at their best when they are with their peers, Annual days, Sports Day and other cultural activities are one such platform where children work in groups, learn the importance of team work, display their creative talent and perform fearlessly on the stage.

Celebrations

Celebrating festivals and events is the most awaited part since children get an opportunity to understand the diversified culture, we all live in and help in instilling a stronger cultural belief in the minds of our young learners.

Nutrition & Hygiene

We serve nutritious meals and snacks which is cooked at our in-house kitchen to promote healthy eating habits. Keeping the surroundings clean and following all the important norms to keep the premises hygiene
