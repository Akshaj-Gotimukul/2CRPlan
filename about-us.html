Our Campus

Our campus at Kokapet is designed to accommodate 4,000 students.

Our impressive 5-acre campus in Kokapet boasts of first-rate classrooms with ample ventilation, creating a comfortable learning environment. The spacious science laboratories hold the latest equipment and create a curiosity-perking and inspiring environment. The library holds an extensive range of books meant to excite, empower, and invigorate a desire to learn among the students. The school also has WiFi enabled computer labs, digitized classrooms with Interactive Panels, and employs the UOLO app to communicate with parents.

The Pre-Primary campus is aesthetically designed, and provides a clean and hygienic environment. The children’s favorite part of our school is the imaginative and creative toddler indoor play area. We possess a resource center, a children’s library, WiFi accessible computer lab, a play gym and a splash pool for the children to enjoy; a great combination for all the hands-on and fun based learning for the Kindergarteners.

Our Cambridge building, nestled within our 5-acre campus in Kokapet, offers an exceptional learning environment. With well-ventilated classrooms and state-of-the-art facilities, including advanced science laboratories and a well-stocked library, we aim to foster curiosity and a passion for learning. Additionally, our Cambridge building features digitized classrooms with Interactive Panels, and seamless communication with parents through the UOLO app. It provides the perfect setting for students to excel in their Cambridge curriculum and explore their full potential.
